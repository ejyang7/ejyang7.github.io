About Me

Hi there! I am a Master's student studying Computer Science at Stanford University with interests in both Artificial Intelligence and Human-Computer Interaction. Ultimately, I want to be able use my creative background and knowledge in Computer Science to design and develop products to benefit consumers.

Prior to my move to the Bay Area, I graduated from the University of Maryland, College Park in May 2018 with a double major in Finance and Information Systems. After graduating, I moved to New York City and worked at Deloitte for two years in IT Risk before deciding to switch fields into CS.

In my free time, I love being active with volleyball, wushu (a performance-based Chinese martial arts), and hiking. I also enjoy dabbling in anything art-related, traveling, and any and all aspects of food (whether it's food adventures, eating, cooking, or baking).

Projects

Simple Yelp Clone

Displays a list of search results from the Yelp API in a scrollable list.

Language: Kotlin

My Maps (Simple Google Maps)

Displays a list of maps, each of which show user-defined markers with a title, description, and location. The user can also create a new map to add to the list with the above markers.

Language: Kotlin

Tippy

Computes the tip and total amount for a bill. The app uses the base amount and tip percentage to calculate the amount owed, and it also describes the quality of service based on the tip. In addition, if the user specifies the number of people in the party, the app will also split the bill evenly and calculate the amount each person would pay.

Language: Kotlin
